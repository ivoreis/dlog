# Trading

- Trading & Brokerage (Golden goose) (35% - 50% Global Revenues)
	- Brokerage (Market making) - Intermediary services
		- Trade on behalf of clients and profit from a bid/ask spread
		- Asset Managers buy securities for the long-term
	- Proprietary Trading 
		- uses bank's own money to buy securities and sell them at a profit
		- Buy and sell stocks in order to profit from capital gain
		- Short-term horizon
		- Types:
			- Shares
			- Bonds
			- Derivatives (Mostly for hedging purposes and reduce risk or speculative)
				- Forwards
				- Futures
				- Options
        - Swaps
